## Variation coverage notes

- Business models: usage/API (Exa, E2B, Terminal49), subscription SaaS (Freed, SchoolAI), marketplaces/take-rate (Posh, Pickle, Bezel, Chowdeck, Cargado), transaction/interchange (Straddle, Felix Pago, Kudos), RaaS/hardware (Chef Robotics, Matic, Contoro), registry/verification fees (Isometric), care delivery/reimbursement (Millie, Fay, Legion Health), open-core (Langfuse, Chroma, Tobiko, Formance).
- GTM: developer-led (batch 01, 05), enterprise sales (Anterior, Herald, Sereact), PLG/bottoms-up (Freed, Fyxer, Dock), consumer (batch 07, Ello), channel/embedded (Mercoa, Layer, Ansa).
- Operating models: pure software, hardware-dependent (batch 09), manufacturing-dependent (Daedalus, Isembard), regulated (batch 03, Gradient Labs, Straddle), physical-operations (Chowdeck, Lithos).
- Expected recommendation spread: agents underwrite honestly; the set deliberately includes crowded categories (AI SDR, AI scribes, teacher copilots), capital-intensive models, and consumer retention risks so Watch/Price-sensitive/Pass labels appear alongside Pursue.
